JOVIS PROJECT BRIEF

OVERVIEW

Jovis is a medical and wellness app designed to give users the freedom to manage all of their health needs under one app. The application offers users features such as the option to explore health content, track habits, book medical appointments, message other users and professionals, and much more. It was intended to be a source of support for busy individuals to improve their health, as well as a point of motivation for those who need held stsarting and maintaining a health routine.

Problem

How do we design an all-inclusive health and wellness app for those who lead busy lives on-the-go?

Objective

Create a health application that prioritizes an individual's success and motivates them with an intuitive design that allows them to not only access doctors, book appointments, & update their medical records, but take their health and well-being knowledge into their own hands.

DESIGN PROCESS

DISCOVERY

In order to better understand the market of health and wellness apps, I conducted two competitive analysis with high-ratings and fast growing user bases. I also conducted user interviews and surveys to understand the needs and wants of potential users.

USABILITY TESTING

After finishing my initial prototype, I conducted three rounds of usability testing, and compiled observations, errors, thoughts, etc. into affinity maps as well as a spreadsheet to help me brainstorm solutions.

FINAL DESIGN

Based on the feedback from the usability testing, as well as peer reviews, I reiterated my designs until I had a prototypes that was accessible, responsive, and user-friendly.

After completing more screens and adding more decorative elements and features, I hope that users will respond positively the general feeling of the app, as well as the ease of having a fully-functional, all-around health and wellness app.
